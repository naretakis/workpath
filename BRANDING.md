## Brand Voice and Tone

### Voice Characteristics

- **Supportive**: We're here to help, not judge
- **Clear**: Plain language, no bureaucratic jargon
- **Empowering**: You're in control of your data and compliance
- **Respectful**: Dignity-focused, stigma-free language

### What We Say

✅ "Track your hours"
✅ "Maintain your benefits"
✅ "Stay compliant"
✅ "Your data, your control"
✅ "We're here to help"

### What We Avoid

❌ "Welfare requirements"
❌ "Mandatory reporting"
❌ "Surveillance"
❌ "Monitoring"
❌ Judgmental or punitive language
